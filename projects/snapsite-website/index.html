Features

Everything a field crew needs, nothing they don't

From the first tap of the shutter to the lead's final approval, SnapSite keeps the whole job in one organized place.

GPS-tagged photo grouping

Every photo is stamped with its location and automatically grouped into the right project the moment it's taken — no manual sorting back at the truck.

Workgroups with lead visibility

Inspectors capture their own sites while the lead account sees everything — every inspector, every location — in one consolidated view.

Flexible photo sharing

Share with the whole workgroup, hand-pick specific inspectors, or generate a public link for clients and contractors — you decide the audience.

Voice & text notes + AI reports

Add a voice or text note to any photo. SnapSite's AI drafts a clean inspection report from your notes — but nothing saves until the inspector reviews and approves it.

Interactive map view

See all active projects as pins on a map. Tap any pin to jump straight into that site's photos, notes, and status.

Complete & approval workflow

An inspector marks a project done; the lead approves a one-tap close-out that archives the photos to your destination and clears it off the active list.

One-tap camera, always a thumb away

A persistent quick-access camera button means a new photo is one tap from anywhere in the app — captured, tagged, and filed before you've taken your hand off the wall.

How it works

From shutter to sign-off in four steps

1

Snap on site

Tap the camera. Photos are GPS-tagged and dropped into the matching project automatically.

2

Annotate & draft

Add voice or text notes. AI drafts the report; the inspector reviews and approves before anything is saved.

3

Share & review

Share with the workgroup, specific inspectors, or a public link. The lead tracks every site from the map.

4

Close it out

Inspector marks it done, lead approves the one-tap close-out, and photos archive to your destination.
